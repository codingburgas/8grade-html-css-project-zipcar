Rental policies

Rental Requirements

  • Minimum age of 25 years for all luxury vehicle rentals
  • Valid driver's license held for at least 3 years
  • Credit card in the renter's name for security deposit
  • Proof of insurance or purchase of our insurance coverage
  • Additional identification may be required

Insurance & Coverage

  • Basic insurance included in all rental rates
  • Premium coverage available for additional fee
  • Security deposit required, amount varies by vehicle
  • Deposit refunded upon vehicle return in same condition
  • Additional drivers must meet all rental requirements

Reservation & Cancellation

  • Advance reservation recommended, especially during peak seasons
  • Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pickup
  • 50% charge for cancellations within 24-48 hours of pickup
  • No refund for cancellations less than 24 hours before pickup
  • No-shows will be charged the full rental amount

Vehicle Return

  • Return vehicle with same fuel level as at pickup
  • Late returns charged at hourly rate plus penalty fee
  • Vehicle inspection conducted upon return
  • Additional charges for excessive cleaning required
  • 24/7 return available at our main location

Additional Information

Mileage Policy

All rentals include unlimited mileage within Scotland. For travel outside Scotland, please inform us in advance as additional charges may apply.

Fuel Policy

All vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and should be returned with a full tank. If not, a refueling charge plus service fee will be applied.

Additional Services

We offer various additional services including GPS navigation, child seats, additional drivers, and chauffeur service. Please inquire about pricing when making your reservation.
